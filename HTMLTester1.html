What is space?

Space is defined as the area outside of Earth's atmosphere, and it includes billions of stars, moons, planets, galaxies, and even more bodies.

Space is a wonderfully terrifying subject, as only 4% of the visible parts of the universe has been discovered.

Space will continue to be further researched, and advancements in science, technology, and math will play a huge role in how space research is conducted.
