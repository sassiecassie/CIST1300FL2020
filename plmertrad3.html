About

Glenn Palmer began working with his dad, Jerry Palmer, at the age of 12. While working for his dad, Glenn learned all of the vital skills to start his career in custom home building, completing his first house at age 22.

Glenn continues to stay up to date with certifications and builder trends, utilizing his skills to perfect his work.

Glenn is a proud father of 4 children, and he hopes to pass on the custom home building tradition to one of his kids.
